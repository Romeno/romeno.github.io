Localização

Suceava is a city in northeastern Romania, located in the historical region of Bukovina. It was the capital of the Principality of Moldavia between 1388 and 1565. Today, it's an important cultural and historical center, known for landmarks like the Suceava Fortress and nearby painted monasteries, which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The city also serves as a gateway to the scenic landscapes of the Carpathian Mountains. Suceava has a mix of historical architecture and modern development, reflecting its long-standing significance in Romanian history.
